David Get Keen

WISHLIST ON STEAM

Release: Coming Soon
Genre: 3d Platformer


The journey is everything

David Get Keen is a fast paced 3d platforming game, where your interactions with the environment dictate your speed and the effectiveness of your traversal. As you progress through the game, you will learn the multi-purpose nature of all the game's moves, and discover faster methods to progress the levels. THIS GAME CONTAINS EXACTLY 0 INVISIBLE WALLS, AND EXACTLY ONE SWEAR WORD


Features

  • Flexible character moveset
  • Powerful horizontal and vertical traversal
  • Linear yet open ended levels
  • Breakneck speeds
  • Awful cinematics
  • Stupid characters
